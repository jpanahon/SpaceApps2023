About the Project

Welcome to our website! We are a passionate team of university students dedicated to raising awareness about the conservation of endangered species. Our project began as a collective endeavor to share knowledge and information about these remarkable creatures that share our planet. With a shared commitment to wildlife preservation, we've built this platform to provide a comprehensive database of endangered species, their statuses, and detailed profiles. Our goal is to inspire and educate visitors about the importance of biodiversity and the urgent need for conservation efforts. Together, we believe that by spreading awareness, we can make a positive impact on the future of these incredible species and the ecosystems they call home. Created for the 2023 NASA SpaceApps Challenge!
